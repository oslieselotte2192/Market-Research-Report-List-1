<p><p>The 3D imaging and sensing components market includes various sensing technologies that enhance depth perception and spatial awareness. Stereo sensing utilizes dual cameras to capture depth information by comparing images from different angles. Structured light employs projected patterns to facilitate depth mapping by analyzing distortions. Time of Flight (ToF) technology measures the time it takes for emitted light to return after hitting an object, providing accurate distance data. Each technology offers unique advantages for applications in robotics, gaming, and augmented reality.</p></p>
