<p><p>Adiabatic coolers are heat exchange systems that enhance cooling efficiency while conserving water. The market can be divided into two primary types: dry air coolers and liquid coolers. Dry air coolers utilize ambient air to cool fluids without direct water contact, ideal for environments with limited water supply. Liquid coolers, on the other hand, use a water-cooling medium to enhance thermal exchange, making them suitable for sectors requiring precise temperature control. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, emphasizing energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
