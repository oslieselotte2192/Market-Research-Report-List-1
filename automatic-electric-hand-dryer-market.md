<p><ul><li>Jet Air Type</li><li>Warm Air Dryer (WAD) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic electric hand dryer market comprises primarily two types: jet air type and warm air dryer (WAD) type. Jet air hand dryers utilize high-speed jets of air to rapidly dry hands, usually in 10-15 seconds, promoting hygiene and efficiency. In contrast, warm air dryers blow heated air to evaporate moisture, typically taking 30-45 seconds to dry hands. Both types are eco-friendly alternatives to paper towels, but they cater to different user preferences for speed and comfort in public restrooms.</p></p>
