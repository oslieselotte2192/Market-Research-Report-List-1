<p><ul><li>Monolithic</li><li>Multiple Slices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile leaf springs market comprises two main types: monolithic and multiple slices. Monolithic leaf springs are single, solid pieces of metal, offering simplicity, durability, and cost-effectiveness, often used in heavy-duty vehicles. Multiple slices consist of layered steel strips, providing flexibility, better weight distribution, and improved ride comfort, commonly found in passenger cars and light commercial vehicles. Both types serve to absorb shocks, support vehicle weight, and enhance stability, catering to diverse automotive needs based on load capacity and performance requirements.</p></p>
