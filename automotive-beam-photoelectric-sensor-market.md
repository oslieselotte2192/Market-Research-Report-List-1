<p><strong>The Automotive Beam (Photoelectric) Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Reflective Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive beam (photoelectric) sensor market includes three primary types: through-beam, reflective, and diffuse photoelectric sensors. Through-beam sensors utilize a transmitter and receiver separated by a distance, detecting interruptions in the light beam for precise object detection. Reflective sensors bounce light off a target to identify its presence, often used for proximity sensing. Diffuse sensors emit light and rely on reflections from the detected object itself, making them suitable for short-range applications. Each type serves specific automotive functions, enhancing safety and automation.</p></p>
