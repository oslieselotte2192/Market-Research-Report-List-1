Conversely, cordless scanners use Bluetooth connectivity, providing greater mobility and ease of use, making them ideal for on-the-go diagnostics. Both types serve the automotive industry by enabling technicians to quickly troubleshoot and diagnose vehicle issues, enhancing overall efficiency and vehicle maintenance.</p></p>
