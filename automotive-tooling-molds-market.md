<p><p>The automotive tooling market encompasses various types used for manufacturing components, including stamping dies, casting molds, and plastic molds. Stamping dies are used to shape metal sheets into parts through deformation. Casting involves pouring molten material into molds to create complex shapes, often used for engine components. Plastic molds are designed for producing plastic parts through injection molding or extrusion processes. Other tooling methods may include composite, rubber, or additive manufacturing, catering to diverse automotive manufacturing needs and innovations.</p></p>
