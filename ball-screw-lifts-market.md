<p><ul><li>Rotary Ball Screw</li><li>Translation Ball Screw</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screw lifts are categorized into three main types: Rotary Ball Screws, Translation Ball Screws, and Others. Rotary Ball Screws convert rotational motion into linear movement, ideal for applications requiring high precision and efficiency. Translation Ball Screws, on the other hand, enable linear motion by preventing rotation, often used in vertical lifting systems. The "Others" category encompasses various specialized designs or hybrid systems that combine features from both types, catering to niche applications and specific operational requirements.</p></p>
