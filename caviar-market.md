<p><strong>The Caviar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Malossol Caviar</li><li>Pressed Caviar</li><li>Salted Caviar</li><li>Pasteurized Caviar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Caviar market types include Malossol, Pressed, Salted, and Pasteurized caviar. Malossol, meaning "little salt," features a delicate flavor profile with minimal salt content, enhancing the fish's natural taste. Pressed caviar combines different roe types, creating a unique texture and flavor. Salted caviar is preserved with higher salt content, extending shelf life. Pasteurized caviar undergoes heat treatment, ensuring safety and longer preservation, though it may lose some of its fresh characteristics. Each type caters to distinct consumer preferences and culinary uses.</p></p>
