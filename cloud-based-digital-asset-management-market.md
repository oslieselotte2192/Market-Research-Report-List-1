<p><p>The Cloud Based Digital Asset Management market comprises three primary service models: SaaS, PaaS, and IaaS. SaaS (Software as a Service) offers ready-to-use digital asset management tools accessible via the internet, enabling users to manage and share assets without infrastructure concerns. PaaS (Platform as a Service) provides a framework for developers to build and customize digital asset management applications, while IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) supplies the underlying hardware and storage resources needed to host these solutions, allowing for greater control and scalability.</p></p>
