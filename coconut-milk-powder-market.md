<p><p>The coconut milk powder market comprises two primary types: pure coconut milk powder and mixed coconut milk powder. Pure coconut milk powder is derived solely from fresh coconuts, offering a rich flavor and high nutritional profile, making it popular in vegan and health-conscious products. In contrast, mixed coconut milk powder blends coconut extract with other ingredients, often including starches or additives, to enhance texture, flavor, and shelf life. This variety caters to diverse culinary applications, including soups, sauces, and desserts.</p></p>
