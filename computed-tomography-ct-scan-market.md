<p><p>The Computed Tomography (CT) scan market is categorized into several types based on the technology used. X-Ray Computed Tomography employs x-rays to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body, widely used in medical diagnostics. Ultrasonic Computed Tomography utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create images, primarily focusing on soft tissues. Gamma Rays CT scans leverage gamma radiation to visualize functional processes within the body, often used in nuclear medicine. Each type serves unique diagnostic purposes across various medical fields.</p></p>
