<p><p>Computer Integrated Manufacturing (CIM) encompasses various market types, primarily Computer-Aided Design (CAD), Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM), and Digital Manufacturing (DM). CAD focuses on using software to create precise design models, facilitating efficient product development. CAM, on the other hand, automates manufacturing processes using the designs generated by CAD, optimizing production workflow. Digital Manufacturing integrates advanced technologies and data analytics to enhance operational efficiency, allowing real-time adjustments and smarter decision-making throughout the manufacturing lifecycle. Together, these markets drive innovation and productivity in manufacturing.</p></p>
