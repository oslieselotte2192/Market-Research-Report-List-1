<p><ul><li>Inductive Couplers</li><li>Capacitive Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contactless coupler market primarily includes inductive couplers and capacitive couplers. Inductive couplers use magnetic fields to transfer energy and data between two coils, making them ideal for applications like wireless charging and data transfer in devices. Capacitive couplers, on the other hand, utilize electric fields to connect circuits through dielectric materials, often used in low-power applications. Both types enable efficient communication and power delivery without physical contact, enhancing convenience and reliability in various electronic systems.</p></p>
