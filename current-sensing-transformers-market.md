<p><p>The current sensing transformers market is segmented into three main types: wound, toroidal, and bar transformers. Wound transformers feature coils wound around a core, offering high accuracy and robustness, ideal for various applications. Toroidal transformers have a doughnut shape, providing low electromagnetic interference and compact designs, making them suitable for sensitive electronics. Bar transformers utilize a bar-shaped core, facilitating high current measurements with minimal space requirements, commonly used in industrial settings. Each type caters to specific sensing needs and application demands.</p></p>
