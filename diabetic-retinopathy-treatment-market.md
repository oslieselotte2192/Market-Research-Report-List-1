<p><ul><li>Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (PDR)</li><li>Non-Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (NPDR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diabetic retinopathy treatment market is categorized into two main types: Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (PDR) and Non-Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (NPDR). PDR is characterized by the growth of abnormal blood vessels, requiring advanced treatments like laser photocoagulation or anti-VEGF injections. NPDR, on the other hand, involves milder changes in retinal blood vessels and is often managed with monitoring and laser therapy. Both segments address the severity and progression of diabetic retinopathy, driving innovations and investments in ophthalmic care solutions.</p></p>
