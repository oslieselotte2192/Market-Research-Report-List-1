<p><p>The Disposable Blood Pressure Monitoring Sensor market is categorized into three types based on the number of channels they support. Dual Channel Sensors allow simultaneous measurement of both systolic and diastolic pressures, enhancing accuracy in readings. Triple Channel Sensors offer advanced monitoring capabilities by measuring additional parameters, enabling comprehensive cardiovascular assessments. Each type caters to varying clinical needs and settings, emphasizing efficiency and patient safety.</p></p>
