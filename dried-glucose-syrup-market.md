<p><p>The dried glucose syrup market is categorized into two main types: powder and granular. Powdered dried glucose syrup is finely ground, offering ease of incorporation into various food products, enhancing sweetness and texture without adding moisture. Granular dried glucose syrup, on the other hand, consists of coarser particles, making it suitable for applications requiring longer shelf life and better handling in production processes. Both forms are used widely in the food and beverage industry for their functional benefits, including stability and solubility.</p></p>
