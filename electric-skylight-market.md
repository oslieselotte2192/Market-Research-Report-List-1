<p><ul><li>Pitched Roof</li><li>Flat Roof</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric skylight market is segmented into two main types: pitched roof and flat roof skylights. Pitched roof skylights are designed for sloped roofs, providing optimal natural light and ventilation while enhancing architectural aesthetics. They often feature a sleek design to complement the roof's angle. On the other hand, flat roof skylights are suited for horizontal surfaces and are typically used in modern buildings to maximize daylight without compromising structural integrity. Both types improve energy efficiency and interior ambiance.</p></p>
