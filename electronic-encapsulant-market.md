<p><p>The Electronic Encapsulant Market is segmented into **One Component** and **Multicomponent** types. **One Component** encapsulants are pre-mixed, ready-to-use materials, offering simplicity and ease of application, ideal for small-scale or less complex electronic assemblies. **Multicomponent** encapsulants require mixing before use, providing tailored properties like enhanced thermal or mechanical performance, suitable for advanced or large-scale applications.
