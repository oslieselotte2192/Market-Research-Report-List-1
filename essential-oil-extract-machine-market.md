<p><p>The essential oil extract machine market primarily features hydraulic and screw oil press types. Hydraulic machines use high pressure to extract oils, ensuring maximum yield while preserving the oil's quality and aroma. These are often favored for extracting delicate oils from flowers and herbs. On the other hand, screw oil presses operate through a mechanical screw, providing efficient extraction, especially for seed oils. Both types cater to different extraction needs, with variations in capacity, efficiency, and suitability for various raw materials.</p></p>
