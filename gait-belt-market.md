<p><p>The gait belt market features various types including autobuckle, quick release, and side release mechanisms. Autobuckle belts offer automatic tightening without manual adjustments, ensuring a secure fit for users. Quick release belts provide ease of removal with a simple push-button mechanism, enabling swift response in emergencies. Side release belts feature a latch that opens from the side, allowing for convenient securing and unfastening. Each type enhances safety and usability for caregivers and patients, facilitating mobility support for individuals with limited mobility.</p></p>
