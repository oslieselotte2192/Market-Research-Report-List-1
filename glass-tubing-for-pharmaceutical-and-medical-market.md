<p><p>Glass tubing for the pharmaceutical and medical market includes two primary types: soda lime glass and borosilicate glass. Soda lime glass is primarily used for standard applications due to its cost-effectiveness and ease of production, but it has limited thermal resistance. In contrast, borosilicate glass, known for its superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, is ideal for laboratory and pharmaceutical applications where durability and safety are essential. Both types play crucial roles in manufacturing vials, syringes, and other medical devices.</p></p>
