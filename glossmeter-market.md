<p><p>The glossmeter market is categorized into three types based on measurement angles. Single angle glossmeters measure sheen at one fixed angle, providing a quick assessment of gloss for specific surfaces. Two-angle glossmeters offer readings at two different angles, allowing for a more comprehensive analysis and greater versatility across various materials. Multi-angle glossmeters measure gloss at several angles, offering detailed insights into surface characteristics, making them ideal for industries requiring precise gloss evaluations across diverse applications.</p></p>
