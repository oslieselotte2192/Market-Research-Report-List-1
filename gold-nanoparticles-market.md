<p><ul><li>Water Soluble Gold Nanoparticles</li><li>Oil Soluble Gold Nanoparticles</li><li>Both Phase Soluble Gold Nanoparticles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gold nanoparticles market is categorized into three main types based on their solubility: water-soluble, oil-soluble, and both-phase soluble gold nanoparticles. Water-soluble gold nanoparticles are used primarily in biomedical applications, such as drug delivery and imaging, due to their compatibility with biological environments. Oil-soluble gold nanoparticles are utilized in catalysis and cosmetics, providing enhanced stability in non-aqueous mediums. Both-phase soluble gold nanoparticles offer versatility, enabling applications across various fields, including diagnostics and environmental sensing, bridging the gap between polar and non-polar systems.</p></p>
