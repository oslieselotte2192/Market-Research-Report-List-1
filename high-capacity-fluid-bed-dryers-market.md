<p><strong>The High-Capacity Fluid-Bed Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Fluid-bed Dryer</li><li>Vibrating Fluid-bed Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-capacity fluid-bed dryers are essential in various industries for efficient drying processes. Static fluid-bed dryers utilize a stationary bed of material where hot air passes through, promoting uniform drying. In contrast, vibrating fluid-bed dryers incorporate vibrations to enhance material movement, ensuring even exposure to drying air and reducing drying time. Both types cater to different processing needs, with static dryers typically handling larger batches, while vibrating dryers are effective for finer materials or products requiring quicker processing.</p></p>
