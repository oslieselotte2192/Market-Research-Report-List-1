<p><strong>The Household Hair Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Hair Dryer</li><li>Axial Hair Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household hair dryer market features two primary types: centrifugal and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers utilize a fan that spins to create high-velocity airflow, allowing for quick drying and styling. These are often more powerful and efficient, suitable for thicker hair. In contrast, axial hair dryers use a fan that pushes air directly through the unit, offering a gentler drying experience. They are typically quieter and are ideal for finer hair types, focusing on reduced heat damage.</p></p>
