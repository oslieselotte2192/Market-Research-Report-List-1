<p><ul><li>Medium Frequency Generators</li><li>High Frequency Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Induction heating generators are categorized into medium frequency generators and high frequency generators, each serving distinct applications. Medium frequency generators, typically operating between 1 kHz and 10 kHz, are ideal for processes requiring deeper penetration of heat, such as metal hardening and forging. In contrast, high frequency generators, operating above 10 kHz, are suited for applications needing rapid heating and precision, like brazing and welding. Both types enhance efficiency in industrial heating processes, benefiting various sectors including manufacturing and metallurgy.</p></p>
