<p><p>The industrial grade soda ash market comprises two primary types: dense soda ash and light soda ash. Dense soda ash, characterized by its higher bulk density, is commonly used in glass manufacturing, detergents, and other industrial applications due to its efficiency in shipping and storage. Light soda ash, with a lower density, is primarily utilized in chemical processes, water treatment, and as a pH regulator. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial needs, driving demand across multiple sectors.</p></p>
