<p><p>The interior architectural coatings market is segmented into several types, each offering unique properties. Epoxy coatings are chemically resistant and ideal for high-traffic areas. Polyurethane coatings offer excellent abrasion resistance and a glossy finish. Other coatings may include specialty finishes and texture options, catering to specific aesthetic or functional needs in interior environments.</p></p>
