<p><p>The International PEO (Professional Employer Organization) market primarily consists of PEO and EOR (Employer of Record) services, facilitating global workforce management. PEOs enable companies to share employer responsibilities, handling payroll, benefits, and compliance in specific regions. In contrast, EOR services allow businesses to employ individuals in foreign markets without establishing a legal entity, taking on full employer liability. Both models streamline international hiring processes, allowing organizations to expand quickly while ensuring compliance with local labor laws and regulations.</p></p>
