<p><strong>The Ketone Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ketone resins are categorized into two primary types in the market: Type I and Type II. Type I ketone resins, often characterized by their high glass transition temperatures and thermal stability, are commonly utilized in coatings and adhesives, delivering excellent chemical resistance. Type II resins, on the other hand, are known for their lower viscosity and better flow properties, making them suitable for use in inks and paints. Both types serve distinct applications across various industries, driven by their unique properties.</p></p>
