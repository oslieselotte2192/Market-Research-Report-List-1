<p><p>The Lactulose Syrup market can be categorized based on production methods: chemical synthesis and enzymatic method. In contrast, the enzymatic method utilizes specific enzymes to convert lactose into lactulose, providing a more natural approach, often resulting in higher purity and fewer by-products. Both methods cater to varying consumer preferences and regulatory standards in the pharmaceutical and food sectors.</p></p>
