<p><p>The laser brazing machine market encompasses various welding techniques, including fillet welding and lap welding. Fillet welding involves joining two surfaces at a right angle, creating a triangular weld that offers strong structural integrity, ideal for automotive and heavy machinery applications. Lap welding, on the other hand, overlaps two metal pieces and applies heat to the joint, providing a robust bond suitable for sheet metal assemblies. Both techniques leverage laser technology for precision and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
