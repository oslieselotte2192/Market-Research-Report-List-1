<p><p>The liquid deicer market consists of three main types: propylene glycol-based, ethylene glycol-based, and other formulations. Propylene glycol-based deicers are widely used for their low toxicity and environmental friendliness, making them suitable for sensitive areas. Ethylene glycol-based deicers are effective in extreme cold conditions but are more toxic, limiting their application in certain environments. Other deicer formulations may include alternative chemicals or blends, designed to enhance performance, reduce corrosion, or improve application efficiency for various climates and surfaces.</p></p>
