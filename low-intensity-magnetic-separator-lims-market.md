<p><p>The Low Intensity Magnetic Separator (LIMS) market comprises two main types: dry LIMS and wet LIMS. Dry LIMS utilizes a dry feed process, often employed in mineral processing where moisture content needs to be minimized. It is effective for separating ferromagnetic materials from non-magnetic substances. Conversely, wet LIMS operates with a slurry feed, enhancing separation efficiency for fine particles in mineral processing. Both types are crucial in various industries, including mining, recycling, and waste management, for effective separation of magnetic materials.</p></p>
