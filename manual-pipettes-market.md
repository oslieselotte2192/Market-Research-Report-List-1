<p><strong>The Manual Pipettes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multichannel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual pipettes market is primarily segmented into two types: single-channel and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed for precise liquid handling in individual sample volumes, ideal for tasks requiring accuracy in experiments. In contrast, multichannel pipettes allow simultaneous aspiration and dispensing across multiple samples, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput settings such as ELISA assays or PCR preparations. Both types cater to specific laboratory needs, with single-channel pipettes focusing on precision and multichannel pipettes aimed at increasing productivity.</p></p>
