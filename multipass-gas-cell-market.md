<p><strong>The Multipass Gas Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pfund Cell</li><li>White Cell</li><li>Herriott Cell</li><li>Circular Multipass Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multipass gas cells are used for enhanced gas analysis by allowing multiple passes of light through a sample, increasing interaction length. The Pfund cell features a simple linear design, ideal for straightforward applications. White cells utilize mirrors for increased path length in compact spaces, optimizing sensitivity. Herriott cells employ curved mirrors for effective multipath configurations, suitable for high precision tasks. Circular multipass cells offer continuous circular paths, providing efficient light usage in gas analysis. Each type serves unique analytical needs in various industries.</p></p>
