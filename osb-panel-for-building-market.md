<p><p>Oriented Strand Board (OSB) panels are categorized into four market types based on their structural performance and application suitability. OSB/1 is designed for interior use in dry conditions. OSB/2 is suitable for load-bearing applications in dry environments. OSB/3 is developed for load-bearing purposes in humid conditions, while OSB/4 offers enhanced performance for heavy-duty, load-bearing applications in high moisture environments. These classifications help builders select the appropriate panel for their construction needs, ensuring durability and efficiency.</p></p>
