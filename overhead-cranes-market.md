<p><p>The overhead cranes market primarily encompasses two types: single-girder and double-girder cranes. Single-girder overhead cranes utilize a single beam to support the hoist, making them more compact and cost-effective, ideal for lighter loads and facilities with limited space. In contrast, double-girder overhead cranes consist of two beams, providing higher lifting capacities and greater stability, suitable for heavy-duty applications in large industrial settings. Both types enhance material handling efficiency in various industries by optimizing workspace and improving operational productivity.</p></p>
