<p><p>Phase shifters can be categorized into two main types: analog and digital. Analog phase shifters offer continuous phase control, allowing for fine adjustments in signal phase, which is crucial in applications like radar and telecommunications. Digital phase shifters, on the other hand, provide discrete phase shifts and enable precise control through digital signals, making them ideal for modern communication systems and electronically scanned antennas. The choice between them depends on the specific requirements of frequency, bandwidth, and application.</p></p>
