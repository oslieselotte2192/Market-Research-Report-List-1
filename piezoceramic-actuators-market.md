<p><p>The piezoceramic actuators market is categorized into several types based on their design and application. Multilayer actuators consist of multiple piezoelectric layers bonded together, offering high displacement and force, ideal for precise control. Stacked actuators feature layered construction for enhanced performance in compact spaces. Shear actuators use shear mode for lateral movement, providing unique motion capabilities. Amplified actuators incorporate mechanical amplification to increase displacement, making them suitable for applications requiring larger stroke lengths and force output.</p></p>
