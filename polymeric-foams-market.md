<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Foams</li><li>Open Cell Foams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymeric foams are lightweight materials with cellular structures, categorized into closed-cell and open-cell foams. Closed-cell foams have sealed, non-interconnected cells, offering high strength, low moisture absorption, and excellent thermal insulation, making them ideal for construction, automotive, and packaging. Open-cell foams feature interconnected cells, providing softness, flexibility, and sound absorption, commonly used in cushioning, filtration, and acoustic applications. Both types are derived from polymers like polyurethane, polystyrene, and polyethylene, tailored for specific performance requirements across industries.</p></p>
