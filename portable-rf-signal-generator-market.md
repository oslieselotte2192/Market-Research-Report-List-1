<p><p>The Portable RF Signal Generator market features two primary types: Analog Signal Generators and Vector Signal Generators. Analog Signal Generators produce continuous waveforms, offering basic signal generation for testing and troubleshooting in various applications such as communications and broadcasting. In contrast, Vector Signal Generators generate complex modulated signals, enabling testing of modern communication systems by producing real-world scenarios. These devices are essential for designing, testing, and validating the performance of RF and wireless systems, meeting diverse industry demands.</p></p>
