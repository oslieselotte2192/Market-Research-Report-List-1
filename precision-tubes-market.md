<p><p>The precision tubes market is divided into two main types: seamless and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are produced without any seams or joints, offering enhanced strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, welded tubes are formed by welding flat strips of metal, allowing for cost-effective production and versatility in various sizes and shapes. Each type serves distinct industries, with seamless tubes often used in automotive and aerospace, while welded tubes are prevalent in construction and plumbing applications.</p></p>
