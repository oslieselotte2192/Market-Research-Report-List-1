<p><p>Radiation processing in the food industry utilizes ionizing radiation to extend shelf life and enhance food safety. The Gamma-based market employs gamma rays from isotopes like Cobalt-60, effectively penetrating packaging and sterilizing products. In contrast, the Accelerator-based market utilizes electron beams or X-rays generated by accelerators, offering faster processing times and reduced energy use. Both methods aim to eliminate pathogens and pests, improve quality, and ensure longer-lasting food products, catering to diverse consumer needs and safety regulations.</p></p>
