<p><p>The rear view mirror replacement market is primarily segmented into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) Cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) Cameras. CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and low light performance, making them suitable for high-resolution applications. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and cost-effectiveness, which enhances their appeal for mass-market use. Both technologies aim to improve driver visibility and safety through advanced imaging solutions integrated into rearview mirror systems.</p></p>
