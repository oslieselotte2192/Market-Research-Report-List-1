Polyamide (PA) offers high barrier properties, and Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) enhances oxygen barriers, making it suitable for food packaging. Other materials may include biodegradable options or specialized coatings, broadening the scope for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
