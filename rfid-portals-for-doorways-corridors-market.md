<p><p>RFID portals for doorways and corridors utilize various frequency types to enhance access control and asset tracking. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) systems operate at longer ranges, ideal for tracking multiple items simultaneously. High Frequency (HF) and Near Field Communication (NFC) systems support secure data exchange within shorter distances, making them suitable for contactless payments and access control. Low Frequency (LF) systems offer reliable close-range tracking but with lower data rates. Dual Frequency systems combine benefits, providing versatility for diverse applications in security and asset management.</p></p>
