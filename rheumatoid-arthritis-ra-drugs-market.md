<p><strong>The Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA) Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA) drugs market encompasses several key types. NSAIDs reduce inflammation and pain but do not alter disease progression. Analgesics provide pain relief without anti-inflammatory effects. Corticosteroids quickly control inflammation but are limited long-term due to side effects. DMARDs modify disease progression and prevent joint damage. Biological therapies target specific immune responses to reduce RA symptoms and progression. JAK-inhibitors block enzymatic pathways involved in inflammation, offering a newer oral treatment option that also modifies disease activity.</p></p>
