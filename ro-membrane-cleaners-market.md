<p><ul><li>Acid Membrane Cleaners</li><li>Alkaline Membrane Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO membrane cleaners market is divided into two primary types: acid membrane cleaners and alkaline membrane cleaners. Acid membrane cleaners are designed to remove inorganic scales, such as calcium and magnesium deposits, from membranes, enhancing their performance and lifespan. In contrast, alkaline membrane cleaners target organic fouling, effectively breaking down substances like oils and biological contaminants. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining reverse osmosis systems, ensuring optimal efficiency and prolonging the operational life of membranes.</p></p>
