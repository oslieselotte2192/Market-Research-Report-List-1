<p><p>The sand filter market is primarily divided into two types: vertical media filters and horizontal media filters. Vertical media filters feature a tall, upright design, allowing for efficient space utilization and gravity-driven water flow, making them suitable for areas with limited land. In contrast, horizontal media filters have a wide, flat design, promoting slower water flow and enhanced filtration efficiency, ideal for larger treatment capacities. Both types are essential for removing impurities in water treatment processes, catering to diverse industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
