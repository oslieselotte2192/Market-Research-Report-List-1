<p><p>The sliding miter saw market is divided into two main types: single bevel and double bevel. Single bevel saws can tilt in one direction, making them suitable for basic cutting tasks, while double bevel saws can tilt both left and right, allowing for more versatile and complex cuts without repositioning the material. Double bevel models are generally favored by professionals for their increased functionality, whereas single bevel saws are popular among DIY enthusiasts for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
