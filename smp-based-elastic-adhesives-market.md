<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMP-based elastic adhesives market is categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, offering convenience and ease of application, typically curing upon exposure to moisture. In contrast, two-component adhesives necessitate the mixing of a resin and a hardener prior to application, allowing for more control over the curing process and enhancing performance in demanding applications. Both types are valued for their flexibility, durability, and strong bonding capabilities in various industries.</p></p>
