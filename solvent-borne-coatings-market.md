<p><p>The solvent-borne coatings market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component solvent-borne coatings are ready-to-use formulations that do not require mixing before application, simplifying the process and enhancing convenience. In contrast, two-component systems require the mixing of a resin and a hardener, providing superior durability and performance properties, making them suitable for demanding applications. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, industrial, and architectural, due to their effective protective and aesthetic qualities.</p></p>
