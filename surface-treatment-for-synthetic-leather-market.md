<p><p>Surface treatment for synthetic leather involves applying various agents to enhance durability, appearance, and functionality. Solventborne agents utilize organic solvents for effective penetration and bonding, providing a robust finish. Waterborne agents, on the other hand, are environmentally friendly, offering lower VOC emissions while ensuring good adhesion and flexibility. UV agents utilize ultraviolet light for curing, resulting in a hard, resilient surface that resists wear and fading. Together, these treatments improve synthetic leather's performance and aesthetic appeal in various applications.</p></p>
