<p><p>The thermal oil heaters market encompasses various types, notably tubular heating and local rapid heating. Tubular heating involves circulating thermal oil through tubes, ensuring consistent heat transfer for industrial processes. This method is efficient for providing uniform heating in large systems. Local rapid heating, on the other hand, focuses on quickly heating specific areas or equipment, enhancing responsiveness in processes that require immediate thermal input. Both types play vital roles in optimizing energy use and improving operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
