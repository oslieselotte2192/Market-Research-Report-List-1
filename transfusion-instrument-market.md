<p><p>The transfusion instrument market encompasses various types of devices used for delivering fluids and medications. Volumetric infusion pumps precisely control fluid flow rates, ensuring accurate dosing during transfusions and reducing the risk of complications. In contrast, gravity-delivered infusion pumps rely on gravity to regulate flow, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution for fluid administration, though with less precision. Both types play crucial roles in patient care by ensuring safe and efficient transfusion management in healthcare settings.</p></p>
