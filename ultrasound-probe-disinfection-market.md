<p><p>The ultrasound probe disinfection market encompasses various types of probes essential for medical imaging. Phased array probes are compact, commonly used in cardiac imaging, allowing for detailed heart evaluations. TEE probes are specialized for transesophageal echocardiography, delivering critical cardiac information. Other probes include specialized designs catering to specific diagnostic needs, driving innovation in disinfection methods.</p></p>
