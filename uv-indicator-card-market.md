<p><strong>The UV Indicator Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UVA</li><li>UVB</li><li>UVC</li><li>UVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV indicator card market is segmented into four main types based on the ultraviolet spectrum: UVA, UVB, UVC, and UVD. UVA indicators measure long-wave UV radiation, primarily responsible for skin aging. UVB indicators focus on medium-wave UV radiation, linked to sunburn and skin cancer. UVC indicators detect short-wave UV radiation, mainly from artificial sources and known for germicidal properties. UVD indicators represent a broader range, indicating overall UV exposure, catering to diverse applications in skincare, safety, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
