<p><p>The vacuum boosters market includes various types of pumps, each suited for specific applications. Oil-sealed pumps provide efficient, high-performance vacuum levels, ideal for industrial processes requiring low contamination. Water-ring pumps utilize a rotating liquid (typically water) to create vacuum, offering a simple design and environmental benefits, making them suitable for handling vapors and gases. Dry vacuum pumps operate without liquids, reducing maintenance and contamination risks, making them ideal for sensitive applications in pharmaceuticals and electronics.</p></p>
