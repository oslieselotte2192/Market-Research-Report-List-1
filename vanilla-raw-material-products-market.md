<p><p>The vanilla raw material products market is categorized into four primary types: beans, extracts, powders, and pastes. Vanilla beans are the whole pods used for direct flavoring. Extracts, typically made by soaking beans in alcohol, offer a concentrated flavor ideal for baking. Powders are ground forms of beans or extracts, used in both sweet and savory dishes for ease of use. Pastes combine ground vanilla beans and extracts, providing a strong flavor and texture, suitable for a variety of culinary applications.</p></p>
