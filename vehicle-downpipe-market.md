<p><strong>The Vehicle Downpipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Catted</li><li>Catless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle downpipe market is divided into catted and catless types. Catted downpipes include a catalytic converter, reducing harmful emissions and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. They offer a balance between performance and eco-friendliness, making them ideal for street-legal vehicles. Catless downpipes, on the other hand, remove the catalytic converter, maximizing exhaust flow and boosting engine performance. However, they increase emissions and are often restricted for road use, making them popular in racing or off-road applications. Both cater to different needs—catted for legality and catless for performance.</p></p>
