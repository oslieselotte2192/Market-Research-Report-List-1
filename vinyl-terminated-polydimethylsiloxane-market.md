<p><p>Vinyl-terminated polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) is categorized by molecular weight into three market types: Below 500, 500-5000, and Above 5000. Below 500 refers to low molecular weight PDMS, offering high reactivity and fluidity, ideal for coatings and adhesives. The 500-5000 range represents medium molecular weight PDMS, balancing flexibility and stability, commonly used in sealants and lubricants. Above 5000 denotes high molecular weight PDMS, providing superior durability and elasticity, suitable for high-performance applications like medical devices and industrial materials. Each type caters to specific industry needs based on its properties.</p></p>
