<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water pressure booster pump market is categorized into single-stage and multiple-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps consist of one impeller, making them suitable for low-pressure applications, such as residential systems. They are compact and easier to install. Multiple-stage pumps feature multiple impellers, enabling them to generate higher pressure, ideal for industrial and agricultural uses where significant elevation changes are required. The choice between the two depends on specific pressure needs and application environments, influencing overall efficiency and performance.</p></p>
