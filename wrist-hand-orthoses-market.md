<p><p>The wrist-hand orthoses market comprises dynamic and static orthotics, each serving specific therapeutic needs. Both types are essential in treating conditions like carpal tunnel syndrome, tendon injuries, and post-surgical recovery, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
